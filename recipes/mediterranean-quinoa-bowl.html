A colorful, nutrient-dense bowl inspired by Mediterranean cuisine. Packed with plant protein, healthy fats, and fresh vegetables â€“ this lunch will keep you satisfied for hours.

Ingredients

For the Base:

  • 1 cup quinoa, uncooked
  • 2 cups vegetable broth (low sodium)

For the Bowl:

  • 1 cucumber, diced
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/2 red onion, finely chopped
  • 1/2 cup kalamata olives
  • 1/2 cup feta cheese (skip for vegan)
  • 1 can chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  • Fresh parsley, chopped

For the Dressing:

  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Cook quinoa: Rinse quinoa thoroughly. Add to saucepan with vegetable broth. Bring to boil, reduce heat, cover and simmer 15 minutes.
  2. Make dressing: Whisk olive oil, lemon juice, oregano, and garlic together. Season with salt and pepper.
  3. Prep vegetables: While quinoa cooks, dice cucumber, halve tomatoes, chop onion, and drain chickpeas.
  4. Assemble bowl: Start with quinoa as base. Arrange vegetables and chickpeas in sections.
  5. Top and serve: Crumble feta on top. Drizzle with dressing. Garnish with fresh parsley.
