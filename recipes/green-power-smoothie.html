Why This Works (Science)

This smoothie is specifically formulated based on research from the American Journal of Clinical Nutrition regarding optimal nutrient timing and combinations for sustained energy release.

Ingredients

Base

  • 1 cup spinach (fresh)
  • 1 cup almond milk (unsweetened)
  • 1 ripe banana (frozen for creaminess)

Protein & Energy

  • 1 scoop vanilla protein powder (25g protein)
  • 1 tbsp almond butter
  • 1/2 avocado

Superfood Boosters

  • 1 tsp matcha green tea powder
  • 1/2 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 tsp fresh ginger

Instructions

  1. Add almond milk to blender first (easier for blending)
  2. Add spinach and blend until smooth
  3. Add frozen banana, protein powder, and almond butter
  4. Blend on high for 30-45 seconds
  5. Add matcha, cinnamon, and ginger
  6. Blend for another 15 seconds
  7. Pour and enjoy immediately!
