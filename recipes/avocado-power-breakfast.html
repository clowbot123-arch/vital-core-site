Avocado Power Breakfast

Start your day with sustained energy

This nutrient-packed breakfast combines healthy fats, protein, and complex carbs to keep you energized throughout the morning. No mid-morning crash guaranteed!

Ingredients

  • 1 ripe avocado
  • 2 eggs (or 200g silken tofu for vegan)
  • 1 slice whole grain bread (or gluten-free)
  • 1/2 cherry tomatoes
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional: chili flakes, microgreens

Instructions

  1. Prepare the avocado: Cut in half, remove the pit, and scoop out the flesh. Mash lightly with a fork.
  2. Toast the bread: While toasting, heat olive oil in a pan.
  3. Cook the eggs: Fry or poach to your preference. For runny yolks: 3-4 minutes in barely simmering water.
  4. Assemble: Spread mashed avocado on toast. Top with eggs.
  5. Garnish: Add cherry tomatoes, chili flakes, and microgreens.
  6. Season: Salt and pepper to taste. Serve immediately.
