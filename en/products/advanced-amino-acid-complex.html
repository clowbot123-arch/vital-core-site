Why amino acids matter more after 40

As we age, recovery bandwidth shrinks. You can still build and keep muscle, but the margin for error is smaller: sleep quality, stress, protein intake, and training structure matter more. Amino acids are the building blocks your body uses to repair and build muscle protein. If protein intake is inconsistent, or you’re in a calorie deficit, targeted amino acids can help support a more stable recovery routine.
