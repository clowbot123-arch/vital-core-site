Cottage cheese berry bowl

Ingredients (1 serving)

  • 250g cottage cheese (or skyr)
  • 120g mixed berries (fresh or frozen)
  • 20g walnuts, chopped
  • 1 tbsp ground flaxseed
  • 1 tsp honey (optional)
  • Cinnamon to taste

Instructions

  1. Add cottage cheese to a bowl.
  2. Top with berries, walnuts, and flaxseed.
  3. Add cinnamon and a small amount of honey if needed.
  4. Eat immediately, or pack into a container for work.

Practical nutrition angle

This bowl gives a strong protein base in under 5 minutes, making it easier to hit daily protein targets. Berries and flax add fiber for better satiety and blood sugar stability.
