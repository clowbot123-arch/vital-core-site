Protein overnight oats

Prep tonight, eat tomorrow. A high-fiber, high-protein breakfast that keeps energy stable.

Ingredients

  • 60g rolled oats
  • 200g Greek yogurt (or skyr)
  • 150ml milk (or soy milk)
  • 1 tbsp chia seeds
  • 1 tbsp peanut butter
  • 1/2 banana (sliced)
  • Cinnamon + pinch of salt

Instructions

  1. Mix oats, chia, salt, and milk in a jar.
  2. Stir in yogurt and peanut butter until smooth.
  3. Top with banana and cinnamon.
  4. Cover and refrigerate overnight (6‚Äì10 hours).
  5. Eat cold, or warm briefly if you prefer.

Why this works

Oats + chia provide slow carbs and fiber, while yogurt adds protein to support muscle and appetite control.
