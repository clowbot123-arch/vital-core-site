Lemon Ginger Detox Water

Refreshing health booster

Start your morning with this refreshing detox water. Lemon provides vitamin C and aids digestion, while ginger supports metabolism and adds a warming kick.

Ingredients

  • 1 liter filtered water
  • 1 organic lemon (sliced)
  • 1 inch fresh ginger root (sliced)
  • Optional: 1/2 cucumber, sliced
  • Optional: Fresh mint leaves
  • Optional: Pinch of cayenne pepper

Instructions

  1. Prepare ingredients: Wash all produce thoroughly. Slice lemon and ginger.
  2. Combine: Add lemon slices and ginger to a pitcher.
  3. Add water: Fill pitcher with filtered water.
  4. Macerate: Let sit in refrigerator for at least 2 hours (overnight for stronger flavor).
  5. Serve: Pour over ice and enjoy throughout the day.
