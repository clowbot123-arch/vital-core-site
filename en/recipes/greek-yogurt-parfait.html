Greek yogurt parfait

A fast high-protein breakfast or snack with crunch and berries.

Ingredients

  • 250g Greek yogurt
  • 100g berries
  • 30g granola (or oats)
  • 1 tbsp honey (optional)
  • 1 tbsp chopped nuts

Instructions

  1. Add half the yogurt to a bowl or jar.
  2. Layer berries and granola.
  3. Add remaining yogurt.
  4. Top with nuts and a small drizzle of honey if desired.

Why this works

High protein supports muscle and satiety; berries add micronutrients and fiber.
