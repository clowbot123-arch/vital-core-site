Homemade hummus & veggies

Plant-protein snack that works for fat loss: filling, high-fiber, simple.

Ingredients

  • 1 can chickpeas
  • 1 tbsp tahini
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Lemon juice
  • Salt + cumin
  • Carrots + cucumber + peppers

Instructions

  1. Blend chickpeas, tahini, oil, lemon, salt, cumin until smooth.
  2. Add water to adjust texture.
  3. Serve with sliced vegetables.

Why this works

Chickpeas provide fiber and plant protein, which improves satiety and helps calorie control.
