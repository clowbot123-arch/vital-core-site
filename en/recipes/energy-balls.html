No-bake energy balls

Quick snack for stable energy: oats + nut butter + seeds.

Ingredients

  • 100g oats
  • 80g peanut butter
  • 2 tbsp chia seeds
  • 1 tbsp cocoa (optional)
  • 2 tbsp honey
  • Pinch of salt

Instructions

  1. Mix all ingredients in a bowl.
  2. Roll into small balls.
  3. Chill 30 minutes for best texture.
  4. Store in fridge up to 5 days.

Why this works

Fiber + fats help reduce snack cravings and keep energy steady.
