High-Protein Chia Pudding

Creamy meal-prep breakfast with 32g protein and minimal morning effort.

Ingredients

  • 30g chia seeds
  • 200g skyr or Greek yogurt
  • 120ml milk (or soy milk)
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 80g mixed berries
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • Optional: 10g crushed almonds

Instructions

  1. Mix chia seeds, milk, and vanilla in a bowl or jar.
  2. Stir in skyr until fully combined.
  3. Wait 10 minutes, stir again to avoid clumps.
  4. Cover and refrigerate overnight.
  5. Top with berries, cinnamon, and almonds before eating.

Why this recipe works for fat loss over 40

It combines high protein, fiber, and volume to improve satiety while keeping prep friction low. That combination helps adherence, which drives long-term results.
